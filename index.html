Our Story

Ring Connection

Oluwatomilewa (Tomilewa) first met Olumayowa (Mayowa) when a mutual acquaintance introduced them for a printing job. Tomilewa was looking for someone to help her with her brand's printing needs, and Mayowa's expertise was highly recommended. Their initial meeting was all about business, discussing designs, fonts, and deadlines. Tomilewa was impressed by Mayowa's professionalism and attention to detail. As they worked together on several projects, their conversations gradually shifted from mere business to sharing stories and laughter. Tomilewa discovered they had so much in common, from their love of creativity to their passion for entrepreneurship. Their meetings became something she looked forward to, and she found herself feeling drawn to Mayowa's kind and genuine nature. One day, as they were reviewing a design proof, their hands touched, and Tomilewa felt a spark. It was as if time stood still, and all she could think about was the warmth of Mayowa's hand and the kindness in his eyes. From that moment on, their relationship blossomed into something more. They started going on dates, exploring the city, and sharing their dreams and aspirations. Tomilewa was amazed by how much they connected on every level. Mayowa became not only her printer but also her partner, her confidant, and her best friend. As their love grew, so did their business partnership. They collaborated on new projects, and Tomilewa's brand flourished. People would often comment on the beautiful printing work, and she would smile, knowing that the person behind the amazing work was also the one who had captured her heart. Tomilewa and Mayowa proved that sometimes, the most unexpected encounters can lead to the most beautiful love stories. And they were grateful that their chance meeting turned into a lifelong connection.
